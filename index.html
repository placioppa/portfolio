About me

As a Solution Architect with a background in software engineering and a strong focus on aligning technology with real business needs, I have led system transformations and end-to-end implementations across industries such as telecommunications, logistics, and construction. Over the years, I have learned to combine hands-on technical work with a strategic mindset, ensuring that technology choices are both robust and meaningful within the organization context.

My Work

Challenges
  • Aligning multiple teams and stakeholders around common architectural goals.
  • Handling large volumes of unstructured data (20GB+), often non-normalized and geo-referenced with different coordinate systems.
  • Integrating data from external platforms and tools such as OpenComms Designer, QGIS, and other GIS-based services.
  • Ensuring continuous data synchronization after migrations.
  • Managing projects with high rotation/turnover rates while keeping teams stable and productive.
  • Delivering solutions under short deadlines and resource constraints.
  • Balancing immediate delivery needs with long-term system scalability.
Achievements
  • Improved product owners' visibility into their products' maturity by developing a Quality Control module synchronized with LeanIX.
  • Simplified access to technology standards and security policies by integrating Azure AI Services into the company’s documentation platform.
  • Delivered an end-to-end solution covering the integration design, administration, and maintenance of a fiber-optic network engineering process.
  • Successfully led three cross-functional teams, resulting in effective internal collaboration and accelerated professional development
  • Incorporated critical business functionalities into a concrete analysis solution and enhanced the usability of multiple modules
  • Enhanced the integration capabilities of a fiber-optic network engineering company with 3rd-party providers.
  • Boosted the performance and user experience of a license management software within six months.
  • Maintained a six-person team despite 80% turnover in the overall project
  • Ensured the consistency, scalability, and robustness of every solution and module developed
Impact
  • Improved decision-making and governance by providing an updated overview of the architecture compliance
  • Enabled faster and more reliable product releases by reducing alignment gaps
  • Supported business growth through innovative services and new product offerings
  • Reduced operational inefficiencies by automating data migration and synchronization.
  • Improved team collaboration and professional development across multiple cross-functional teams.
  • Ensured long-term system scalability and smoother onboarding by using widely adopted technology stacks.
  • Strengthened business-IT alignment, ensuring technical roadmaps supported strategic objectives.
